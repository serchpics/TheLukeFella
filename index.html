LUKE PARSONS - THE LUKE FELLA

Luke Parsons, also known as The Luke Fella on Instagram, is a 21 year old appretice at the Art Room Tattoo Studio, located on 11 Essex St E, Temple Bar, Dublin 2, Ireland.

Born and raised in tallaght, Luke has always been interested in different art forms, but clearly the one that stood out the most was the one that he dove into, tattooing. Specialised in traditional and anime style tattoos, The Luke Fella aspires to become one of the top artists in these styles, and discover other fields of interest as time goes on.
